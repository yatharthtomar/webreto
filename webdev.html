Page Speed Optimization

The bounce rate of the visitors is very sensitive to the loading time of your website. A loading time of more than 3 seconds could steer away almost half of your potential customers towards your competitors.

If your website takes too long to load, it could be because of –

  • Unorganized code
  • Multiple redirects
  • Taking long on mobile device means that your site is not responsive
  • Far located servers
  • Low quality of web hosting service
  • Large graphic files

Note: the loading time varies from page to page. The level of improvement will depend on the current state & the extent of access we could have.
